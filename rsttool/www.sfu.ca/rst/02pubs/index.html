This is a list of conferences, workshops and journals where RST research tends to appear. Please send information about venues not listed here, or updates, to Maite Taboada (mtaboada @ sfu.ca).

RST and Discourse Studies Workshop series

Series of workshops initially held in Brazil and collocated with other conferences.

  • 2007 - Collocated with the 16th Intercâmbio de Pesquisas em Linguística Aplicada (InPLA), Pontifícia Universidade Católica de São Paulo, Brazil.
  • 2009 - Named "Brazilian RST Meeting", collocated with The 7th Brazilian Symposium in Information and Human Language Technology (STIL), in São Carlos, Brazil.
  • 2011 - 3rd Workshop, "RST and Discourse Studies" was held with The 8th Brazilian Symposium in Information and Human Language Technology (STIL), in Cuiabá, Brazil.
    • http://corpus.iingen.unam.mx/RST_2011
  • 2013 - The 4th workshop "RST and Discourse Studies" was also held within STIL (2013) in Fortaleza, Brazil.
    • http://encontrorst2013.wix.com/encontro-rst-2013
  • 2015 - The 5th workshop "RST and Discourse Studies", collocated with The 31st Conference of the Spanish Association for Natural Language Processing. Alicante, Spain.
    • https://sites.google.com/site/workshoprst2015/
